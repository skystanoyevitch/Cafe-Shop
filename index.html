Welcome !

Sky's Coffee + Dessert Lounge is a dynamic concept that caters to the community. At every location, we serve top quality coffee and Desserts of all Kinds of tasts.

What we stand for

Quality- our mission is to bring you the best quality drinks and desserts. we buy certified organic coffee from local specialty coffee roasters who roast in small batches to perfection. this level of quality comes at a cost to us, but it's an investment in your enjoyment.

Service- we are so grateful that you came to our store and want you to feel like a guest in our home.

Organic - since you are our guest, we care about your health and purchase several organic products.

Community - our goal is to give the community a reason to be proud.

Passion- not just overcaffeination.

Ingredients

Coffee- our coffee beans are freshly roasted for us in a diedrich machine by a local san diego county roaster using the finest quality organic arabica beans from the major coffee growing regions.

Water- to make good coffee, you need good water. We filter the water to remove almost all impurities, then add minerals back in to enhance taste.

Milk - we use rBST-free california milk. organic milk and soymilk available.
